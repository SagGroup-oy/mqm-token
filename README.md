## What MQM Does

MQM is the economic engine of the SAG Group ecosystem:

- **Farm Workers** — 108 workers at Farm #1 (Baladweyne, Somalia) receive wages in MQM
- **Smallholder Farmers** — 30% harvest advance payments delivered via MQM before crop delivery
- **Investors** — Participate in 4 ICO rounds backed by real commodity revenue
- **App Users** — MQM Banking App enables on-chain payments, transfers, and certificates
